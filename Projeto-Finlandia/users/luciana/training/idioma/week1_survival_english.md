### Week 1 — Survival English (60 minutes) / Semana 1 — Inglês de Sobrevivência (60 minutos)

PT: Abaixo está o plano compacto da aula ao vivo de 60 minutos. Marque as caixas ao concluir.
EN: Below is the compact 60-minute live lesson plan. Check the boxes as you complete.

Tempo / Timing
- 0–10 min: Aquecimento / Warm-up (cumprimentos, apresentação curta)
- 10–20 min: Frases-chave de sobrevivência / Core survival phrases
- 20–35 min: Rotina diária / Daily routine (present simple)
- 35–50 min: Simulação de vida na Finlândia / Finland life simulation
- 50–60 min: Revisão + Reflexão / Review + Reflection

Frases essenciais / Essential phrases
- Hi, my name is Luciana. I am from Brazil. I live in Finland.
- Could you repeat, please? / Where is the bus stop?
- I don’t understand. Can you speak slowly?

Vocabulário de hoje / Today’s vocabulary
- transport: bus, ticket, travel card
- shopping: price, card, receipt
- health: doctor, pharmacy, appointment

Atividades / Activities
- [ ] Activity 1: Record & Repeat (gravar 10 frases úteis / record 10 useful phrases)
- [ ] Activity 2: Role Play (loja e ônibus / shop and bus)
- [ ] Activity 3: Life in Finland simulation (perguntar direções / ask directions)
- [ ] Activity 4: My Routine drill (escrever 6 frases / write 6 sentences)
- [ ] Reflection done (3 linhas / 3 lines)

Tarefa rápida / Quick task
- PT: Escreva 3 frases sobre amanhã (I will...). EN: Write 3 “tomorrow” sentences.
