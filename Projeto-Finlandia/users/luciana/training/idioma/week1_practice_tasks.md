### Week 1 — Practice Tasks / Semana 1 — Tarefas de Prática

PT: Tarefas para praticar sozinha em casa.
EN: Independent practice for home.

| Type | Task | Output |
|------|------|--------|
| Speaking | Record a 1-minute video introducing yourself | Upload or note link |
| Listening | Watch one “6-Minute English” episode | Write 5 new words |
| Writing | “My day in Finland” paragraph (8 lines) | Text entry |
| Vocabulary | Create 10 flashcards PT–EN–FI | Screenshot or list |

Extra challenge / Desafio extra
- PT: Descreva um dia de inverno na Finlândia usando 3 adjetivos.
- EN: Describe a Finnish winter day using at least 3 adjectives.
